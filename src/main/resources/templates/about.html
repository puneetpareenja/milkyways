What We Do

Milkyway, an online dessert ordering platform lets customers browse a wide-selection of Vancouver based bakeries while providing broad search categorisation and delivery services. Baked goods include but are not limited to customised cakes, pastries, cupcakes, donuts, pies and croissants. Our website would allow the user to browse through multiple online stores available on the platform, look up various items offered at those stores and then order the desired items to their chosen address at their chosen time.
